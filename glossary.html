Glossary of Terms*

artifact
an (un)intentional record of the process of creation.
artifactual
a structural context for the presentation of artifacts.
documentation
an explicitly intentional record of a process of creation.
influence
the entire range of experiences gathered in life that can't help but find its way into the process of creation.
process of creation
a system that encompasses thinking and acting with varying degrees of focus, often with the side-effect of producing artifacts and documentation.
piece
an instance of documentation with a higher degree of focus.
focus
the containment and directionality of an idea.
duck station
a specific portable recording device.

*These definitions are a living artifact, and thus, are subject to change without notice. ( as is everything )
