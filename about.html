PROJECT: Space Available

PROJECT: Space Available is 13 months of live-performance-installation artist residencies. Nine artists (from Seattle, New York City, Chicago, Halifax, Nova Scotia, Amsterdam, Holland, Copenhagen, Denmark, Berlin, Germany) will have 1-2 months of researching and developing site-specific work in the heart of the Capitol Hill Neighborhood of Seattle.

CodedOval ::: Artifactuals

CodedOval ::: Artifactuals is the title of Jeffrey Alan Huston's PROJECT: space available residency.
It is my intention to explore the process of creation, as well as the codification of emergent compositional elements, as they arise within the physical and emotional space of my time as a PROJECT: Space Available artist in residence.

Jeffrey Alan Huston

JEFFREY ALAN HUSTON is a Seattle based composer and performer. He has collaborated in the creation and performance of works with Degenerate Art Ensemble, and has created and performed scores to accompany choreography by Amelia Reeber, Paige Barnes, John and Anna Dixon, Peggy Piacenza, Northwest Dance Syndrome and Alia Swersky. He is currently exploring the expressive potential of sound in a variety of contexts including the group Dead Hot, as well as his own investigations in song writing, software based instrument design and implementation, and the inseparable influence between all of those things and the spaces in which they occur. Jeff holds a Bachelors of Music from Cornish College of the Arts.
